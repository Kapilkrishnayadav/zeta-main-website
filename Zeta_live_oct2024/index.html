Zeta provides information about safe parking availability in high dense areas to reduce the traffic congestion.

Zeta Helps to overcome
Current Challenges

  • Metro cities in India are facing a growing problem that affects everyone who owns or drives a vehicle.
  • In India road is the most dependable means of transport.
  • It also leads to illegal parking on sidewalks and in other restricted areas, which can be dangerous for pedestrians and cause damage to public property.
  • On street parking causes safety and congestion problems.
  • People are generally in hurry and wrongly parked their vehicle on roadside.

Zeta Process Flow

Driver - just click on search for parking

Parking owner - Space availablility confirmation shared with Driver

Link for location with G - Map and Parking charges will be shared with Driver

OTP to be shared to the parking owner then space alloted for safe parking

Payment to be made for the time used for parking

Vehicle will exit from parking location

Zeta Key Roles/USP

App for Car Onwer

App for parking owner (Vendor)

Admin panel

  • Driver has to search parking space in app.
  • Then The app sends the request to a nearby parking space owner.
  • The owner has to confirm whether space available or not with parking fees.
  • After reaching the destination driver will share the OTP to owner for space confirmation.
  • Then driver will come to parking to park the vehicle on premises.
